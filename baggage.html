BAGGAGE

So how much can you bring with you on the trip?
Let's find out.

How many check in bags can I have?

Each pasenger that has a boarding pass can have 2 check in bags.
NOTE: Make sure that there are no laptop, cellphones, lithuim batteries, matches, lighters, and flammable items such as fireworks, cleaning materials etc in your check in bag, they should be okay to carry with you.

What can I carry on to the plane?

Each passenger can have a carry on (roller luggage 21") and a personal item to take on the plane (i.e. purse, backpack). Check out the TSA check list here to see what you can and cannot bring with you.

How many check in bags can I have if I have an infant traveling with me?

The parent passenger can have 2 check in bags and 1 check in for the infant. An infant can also check in a bassinet and a stroller. CONNECTION FLIGHTS: Tell the check in agent you want to pick up your stroller at the first destination IF YOU NEED IT otherwise it will be sent to your final destination.

What is the weight limit for economy class? Each check in luggage can weigh up to 23kg or 50lbs.
NOTE: The total amount of weight for both luggage can be up to 46kg/100lbs but each luggage can only weigh up to 23kg/50lb EACH. For example your luggage can not be 30kg + 16kg = 46kg. You are subject to a baggage fee if they are overweight.

What is the weight limit for first and business class?

Each check in luggage can weigh up to 32kg or 70lbs.
NOTE: The total amount of weight for both luggage can be up to 64kg/140lbs but each luggage can only weigh up to 32kg/70lb EACH.

How much for an extra bag? How much for 2 extra bags? ETC.

NOTE: Any baggage that is over 32kg/70lbs will not be accepted.
24kg-28kg: $30
29kg-32kg: $75

Does the size of what I check in matter?

Short answer: YES.
Your baggage should not exceed 203cm/80in and is subject to charge.
